03
Security Layers — Anti-scraping Protection
Price Range Display
±5% band around exact premium. Showing $186–$207 instead of $196. Competitors cannot extract a precise number to undercut.
Session Jitter
±3% per-browser offset in localStorage. Each session gets a different range. Averaging attacks fail completely.
Email Gate
Email required before full quote shown. Kills anonymous scraping — every probe is tied to a real email address stored in our DB.
GLM Fallback
Actuarially consistent log-linear formula when backend is offline. Methodologically sound for the thesis. Shows notice.
